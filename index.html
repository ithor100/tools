Code camping in the time of Corona

This is a bit of a braindump and a journal and a way of coping with two major events that entered my life recently.

One was fully planned and accounted for:
Becoming a participant of Code Camp Leipzig for 3 months from mid-March until mid-June 2020.

The other event come out of the blue:
The Coronavirus and the massive unfurling of events and measures that impacted the lives of billions of people around the world.
